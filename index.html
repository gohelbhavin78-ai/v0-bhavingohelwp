FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our financial services.

For a home loan, you typically need: Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN), Address proof, Income proof (salary slips, ITR), Bank statements (last 6 months), Property documents, and passport-size photographs. Additional documents may be required based on employment type.

Loan approval time varies by type. Personal loans can be approved within 24-48 hours, while home loans typically take 7-10 working days. With our streamlined process and bank partnerships, we ensure faster approvals compared to direct applications.

You can start a SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) with as low as Rs. 500 per month. This makes mutual fund investing accessible to everyone. We help you choose the right funds based on your risk profile and financial goals.

Our consultation services are absolutely free. For loan processing, we charge a nominal fee only upon successful disbursement. Investment advisory services are commission-based through AMFI registration, meaning you pay no extra fees.

We have partnerships with all major banks and NBFCs including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis Bank, Bank of Baroda, Bajaj Finserv, Tata Capital, and many more. This allows us to offer you the best rates and terms available in the market.
